Safety Simulation

Revolutionizing industrial safety training with immersive virtual environments.

Project Overview

← Back to Website

Safety Simulation is a Virtual Reality–based industrial safety training system designed around rule-driven interaction and compliance enforcement. The system emphasizes behavioral conditioning by allowing task completion only when mandatory safety protocols are satisfied.

Scenarios

1. Fire Safety: Trains users in extinguisher handling. Fire is extinguished only when aimed correctly and pin is removed.

2. Machine Operation: Enforces PPE compliance. Proximity detection prevents users from approaching heavy machinery without helmets and vests.

3. Biohazard Safety: Simulates controlled access. Entry gates remain locked until the user equips a protective mask.
