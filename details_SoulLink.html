Soul Link: Kalaignar Experience

Resurrecting History: An Interactive Conversation with Dr. M. Karunanidhi

Project Overview

← Back to Website

The Soul Link: Kalaignar Experience is a groundbreaking Virtual Reality demo that integrates advanced AI capabilities to recreate the presence of the late Dr. M. Karunanidhi (Kalaignar).

The core achievement of this project is the synthesis of authentic voice and personality. Users can ask questions about his literary works, his political journey, or his vision for society, and receive intelligent, context-aware responses delivered in his own voice.

Project Gallery

Key Upgrades

  • Authentic Voice Cloning: Utilizes ElevenLabs to replicate Kalaignar’s distinct baritone.
  • Real-time Conversation: Powered by Soul Link’s conversational brain.
  • Legacy Knowledge Base: Fine-tuned on literary works like Kuraloviyam and Thenpandi Singam.

Technology Stack

  • Engine: Unity 3D
  • Brain: Convai
  • Voice: ElevenLabs
